Gina Mata, Gina Al'umma
Building Women, Empowering Communities.

Gina Mata, Gina Al-umma, translated as “Build up women, Build the community”,
is a digital skills development program designed to identify, prepare, and connect
disadvantaged girls and young women with emerging skills in the knowledge
economy. The programme aims to integrate more women into the gig ecosystem
and significantly improve girls' and women’s economic participation in the digital
space
